# description

_Dungeon's Gambit_ is an **incremental-style chessboard crawler**, that **mixes real-time and turn-based** action. 

Unlock new abilities and find the best synergies to find your way through procedurally generated levels,
and fight different chess-inspired enemies and bosses, each with their own unique patterns and abilities.

# about the team

Bipolar Dawn is a one-man studio run by game crafter, Paco Juan, with the objective
of crafting small, curated experiences with the help of fellow developer friends.
After the studio's last title, [_World Soccer Strikers '91_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/877870/World_Soccer_Strikers_91/),
Paco is now teaming up with composer and sound designer, Bibiki García, and
illustrator, David G. Vaquero, to release their newer project: _Dungeon's Gambit_.

#### [Paco Juan](https://www.pacojq.xyz/) <small>[programming; design; 3D and 2D art]</small>

[Paco](https://www.pacojq.xyz/) is an indie and AAA developer, with over ten years of industry experience.
Having participated in multiple indie releases, he's now part of IO Interactive's
engine team, developing the upcoming blockbuster title, [_007 First Light_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3768760/007_First_Light/).
He's been part of the development of other indie titles like [_Deck RX_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1529180/Deck_RX_The_Deckbuilding_Racing_Game/)
and [_Turnip Boy Commits Tax Evasion_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1205450/Turnip_Boy_Commits_Tax_Evasion/).
_Dungeon's Gambit_ is his latest personal project.

#### [José Ramón "Bibiki" García](https://bibikigarcia.com/) <small>[original soundtrack; sound design]</small>

[Bibiki](https://bibikigarcia.com/) is a composer and sound designer, with more than ten years of experience in
the industry. Authoring the soundtrack and sound effects for multiple indie titles,
his resume lists games like [_Arco_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2366970/Arco/),
[_Loco Motive_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1709880/Loco_Motive/) and
[_Alex Kidd in Miracle World DX_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1333470/Alex_Kidd_in_Miracle_World_DX/).

#### [David G. Vaquero](https://davidgvaquero.wordpress.com/) <small>[concept art; commercial art; game logo]</small>

[David](https://davidgvaquero.wordpress.com/) is a colorist and illustrator with vast experience in the comic-book industry.
While his portfolio highlights [book covers](https://davidgvaquero.wordpress.com/illustration/)
and [comic-books](https://davidgvaquero.wordpress.com/color/), he has also worked as
a 2D animator and pixel artist in the games industry, credited in titles like
[_Laika: Aged Through Blood_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1796220/Laika_Aged_Through_Blood/)
and [_Running on Magic_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1961260/Running_on_Magic/).
